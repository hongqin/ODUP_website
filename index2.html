About Hong Qin

Hong Qin, Ph.D., is an associate professor at the School of Data Science, Old Dominion University. His research interests include artificial intelligence, machine learning, computational biology, and aging research. His lab is currently focusing on developing AI tools for pandemic prevention.

Research Interests

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in Healthcare
  • Computational Biology and Genomics
  • Pandemic Prediction and Prevention
  • Aging and Longevity Research
  • Data Science for Public Health
  • Quantum Computing Applications in Biology
